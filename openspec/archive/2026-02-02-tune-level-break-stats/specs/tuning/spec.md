# Specifications: Level Break Tuning

## Requirement: Stat Boosts
The system SHALL apply the following boosts when a "Level Break" occurs (fallback evolution event):
- **Might**: Increase by 0.75.
- **HP**: Increase by 150.
- **Defense**: Increase by 3.

## Requirement: Feedback
The system SHALL heal the player to full HP upon Level Break.
The system SHALL log the power-up details to the console.
